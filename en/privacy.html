Security of your data

Last updated: June 2026.

Application BlueIDE respects your privacy. We designed this product so you can work with peace of mind:

  • Data collection: BlueIDE does not collect, store, or transfer any personal information, traffic logs, or device addresses to third parties.
  • Bluetooth access: Permission to use Bluetooth is requested by the Windows operating system solely for local scanning of the radio air and direct data exchange at your request.
  • Local work:All terminal logs and GATT characteristic data are processed in your PC's RAM and are completely erased when the program is closed.

A direct link to this page fully satisfies the requirements for publishing apps in the Microsoft Store.
