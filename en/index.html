Modern Bluetooth Development Environment for Windows 11

BlueIDE — This is a fast and reliable tool for working with Bluetooth Classic (RFCOMM/SPP) and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE/GATT) technologies. Developed on WinUI 3 for engineers, IoT developers, and electronics makers.

Forget about outdated terminals. BlueIDE provides smart debugging for Arduino, ESP32, and STM32 boards in a modern Fluent Design interface.

Основные характеристики

  • Dynamic scanner: Поиск Bluetooth Classic и BLE устройств в реальном времени.
  • RSSI Radar: Interactive signal strength indicator with distance estimation (Warm/Cold).
  • Smart filtering: Instantly hide unnamed devices and display their MAC addresses.
  • Asynchronous terminal: Reliable bidirectional text exchange of ASCII/UTF-8 strings.
  • [PRO] BLE GATT Analyzer: Reading hidden services, characteristics and descriptors.
  • [PRO] Built-in echo server: Local Bluetooth board simulation for hardware-less loopback testing.
